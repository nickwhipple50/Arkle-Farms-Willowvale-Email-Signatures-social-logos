Instructions to add signature to email

Open the correct page in your browser, then select all of the content on the page using Command+A (Mac) or Ctrl+A (Windows). Once everything is highlighted, copy it with Command+C or Ctrl+C. Then navigate to your email signature settings and paste the content in using Command+V or Ctrl+V.
